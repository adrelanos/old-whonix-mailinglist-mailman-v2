Hi!

WhonixQubes [1], the previous maintainer of qubes-whonix [2]
unfortunately resigned [3].

qubes-whonix is the combination of Qubes and Whonix.

What are the tasks of a maintainer?

- answering questions in the forums [4]
- keeping the wiki [5] up to date
- testing of installation, build, upgrade instructions
- having a look at the issue tracker [6], implementing bug fixes and
perhaps new features in team with nrgaway and other contributors
- otherwise self-initiated (guess)work and care keeping- example current
task: Qubes Whonix 10 - Testing Instructions and Issues [7]

This is a volunteer position.

Forum discussion:
https://www.whonix.org/forum/index.php/topic,1198
